Who We Are

Kilroy Home Services is a family-owned and operated company serving Northeast Ohio for over 20 years. Our mission is simple: provide honest, high-quality home services with a focus on long-term comfort and energy savings for our customers.

Our Story

From humble beginnings offering HVAC and insulation upgrades, we’ve expanded to become a trusted one-stop solution for roofing, siding, windows, doors, and more. What hasn’t changed is our commitment to top-tier workmanship and friendly, local service.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Personalized Solutions – Every home is different, and we tailor our work to your specific needs and budget.
  • Licensed, Bonded, & Insured – You can trust our certified professionals to get the job done safely and correctly.
  • No-Pressure Consultations – We provide honest advice, not pushy sales pitches.
  • Top-Rated Local Support – Most of our customers come from referrals and repeat business.

Community Commitment

We’re proud to be locally owned and actively support causes in the Cleveland community. When you work with Kilroy, you’re supporting your neighbors too.
