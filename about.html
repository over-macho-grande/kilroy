About Kilroy Home Services

Our Story

Kilroy Home Services has proudly served homeowners across Northeast Ohio for over 20 years. What began as a small, family-owned HVAC business has grown into a full-service home improvement company. We specialize in HVAC, insulation, roofing, siding, and window replacements with a commitment to energy efficiency and customer satisfaction.
